About The Conference

Block Party is a hybrid conference for those interested in the future of the internet and disruptive Web3 technologies hosted by Blockchain Student Society.

Multiple industry leaders will be speaking, online and in-person, with topics ranging from Engineering, Business, Computer Science, etc. What do these speakers have in common?

The passion and knowledge of how Web3 tech can impact these industries. Join the hundreds of other like-minded and curious individuals.
